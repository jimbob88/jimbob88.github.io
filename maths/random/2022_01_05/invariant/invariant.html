1 Definitions

Matrix Transformation

A linear transformation represented by a 2x2 matrix.

Invariant Point

A point (coordinate) that is not changed by a matrix transformation.

Invariant Line

A line (in the form y = mx + c), which is not effected by a matrix transformation.

Line of invariant points

A line of points which are individually all invariant points.
